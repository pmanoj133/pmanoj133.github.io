Happiness Score v.s Log GDP per Capita

Another factor is healthy life expectancy at birth. Life expectancy at birth compares the average number of years to be lived by a group of people born in the same year, if mortality at each age remains constant in the future. Life expectancy at birth is also a measure of overall quality of life in a country and summarizes the mortality at all ages. According to the graph we can see that countries have higher happiness score tend to have higher healthy life expectancy at birth. We can see that there may be connections between higher GDP come with better health care, which may leads to higher life expectancy at birth.
